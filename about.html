Hi I'm Ryan!


I am years old living in Asia.

My first experience with programming was when my parents decided to send me to a learning centre to learn Python and Maths when I was 13.

At first, I did not have an interest in the concept of programming. I was learning to draw shapes with Python's Turtle and learn basic geometry repetitively over the course of a few months.

One day, I discovered that I was able to do programming outside the limited bubble restricted by the learning centre; I did not need to use their machines nor their websites!

So along my journey of discovering the potential of Python, I explored the world of "image processing", where I discovered how to use Pillow and OpenCV. Learning OpenCV heavily influenced my journey into programming, since many of the projects I am proud of have OpenCV as a dependency.

I learnt Javascript because a potential client wanted a Discord bot to be fully developed (and already built in Node.js), so I spent 3 weeks learning Node.js, re-applied for the job and got it. And I was inspired to pursue web development by this video, which led to the creation of this website!

I was somewhat known as an avid programmer in my high school. I was appointed to be the first president of my school technology society: Magnus. Being president of The Magnus Society has taught me a lot about the background of organising events, importance of communication with co-workers, presenting in front of a group of people etc.

I have also have a passion of sharing my programming knowledge to other people! I tutored some of my schoolmates in Python, ranging from a private tutoring sessions to a Discord call of 50+ schoolmates watching me stream as I help them with anything they don't understand about Python.

I will continue honing my skills in Python and Javascript, as I continue to explore the world of programming with curiosity and wonder.
